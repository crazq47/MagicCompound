### General Settings ‚öôÔ∏è
üá¨üáß General settings manage the parameters for saving the output image, including its name, format, and storage location.

-   **Index**  ‚Äî the asset layer index, indicating the order in which assets are applied.
-   **Asset**  ‚Äî the asset name, allowing you to predefine its desired index.

-   **Stretch**  ‚Äî how the asset fills the base image. The following options are available:
    -   `None`  ‚Äî default; keeps assets at their original size.
    -   `Fill`  ‚Äî stretches the asset to the full resolution of the target image.
    -   `Uniform`  ‚Äî stretches the asset while preserving its aspect ratio.

-   **VerticalAlignment**  ‚Äî vertical alignment determining the origin for the  **Position**  offset on the Y-axis:
